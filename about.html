About us

Endeavour Welfare Society is a non-governmental organization that came into existence on 15th February 2019 for welfare in the education field. Since then we are playing an important role in supporting the most vulnerable students to access their right to education and build their skillset. Our efforts in all sense support NEP'20 for shifting focus from solely book based learning to skill based practical education.

What we do

We provide placement & apprenticeship opportunities to ITI, BTech, and diploma students for their bright future on a timely basis.While our partner institutes provide schooling and vocational training to the needy and deserving.

National Education Policy

  • The National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020), which was approved by the Union Cabinet of India on 29 July 2020, outlines the vision of India's new education system. The new policy replaces the previous National Policy on Education, 1986.
  • The policy is a comprehensive framework for elementary education to higher education as well as vocational training in both rural and urban India.
