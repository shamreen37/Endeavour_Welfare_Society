About us

The meaning of life is to find your gift. The purpose of life is to give it away.

-Pablo Picasso

We are a non-governmental organization that came into existence on 15th February 2019 for welfare in the education field. Since then we are playing an important role in supporting the most vulnerable students to access their right to education and build their skillset. Our efforts in all sense support NEP'20 for shifting focus from solely book based learning to skill based practical education.
