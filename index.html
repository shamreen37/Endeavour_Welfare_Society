Mission & Vision

“Helping underprivileged and deprived Indian students realize their full potential and achieve optimum growth through education, training, and placement."

The idea is to develop their skills and competencies and make them conversant with the techniques of transferring hands-on skills so today's youth will serve as an asset rather than a liability for society.

Our Services

There is no higher religion than the service of humanity and the satisfaction one gets from helping the nation's future rising star. Here are some of the services we provide to bright young minds to be a difference and to make a difference in society by their skills.

Vocational Training

These courses provide training for the technical and non-technical fields. To provide faster employment opportunities right after class 10th or 12th.

Apprenticeship & Placements

For those who already have prior apprenticeship experience or meet respective job qualifications, we offer jobs and placement in MNCs and leading brands of the nation.

About us

The meaning of life is to find your gift. The purpose of life is to give it away.

-Pablo Picasso

We are a non-governmental organization that came into existence on 15th February 2019 for welfare in the education field. Since then we are playing an important role in supporting the most vulnerable students to access their right to education and build their skillset. Our efforts in all sense support NEP'20 for shifting focus from solely book based learning to skill based practical education.
