Services

There is no higher religion than the service of humanity and the satisfaction one gets from helping the nation's future rising star. Here are some of the services we provide to bright young minds to be a difference and to make a difference in society by their skills.

Vocational Training

These courses provide training for the technical and non-technical fields. What separates our courses from other academic courses is that these aim to provide faster employment opportunities right after class 10th or 12th. The courses are exclusively trading specific, hence they impart essential skills important for the trades like electrician, fitter etc.

Apprenticeship and Placements

Apprenticeships are a great way for young people to gain real-world experience within the industry of their choice. For those who are motivated to start working, apprenticeships are the ideal way of gaining experience in their dream brands such as Maruti Suzuki, MRF, Lupin, Bajaj, Indian railways, Delhi metro etc. For those who already have prior apprenticeship experience or meet respective job qualifications, we offer jobs and placement in MNCs and leading brands of the nation such as TATA, Usha, P&G, Crompton etc.

Important Points

  • Students will be given the opportunity to be flexible in their choice of subjects.
  • There will be no firm division between 'arts', 'academic', 'curricular', 'extra-curricular' or 'vocational' streams to ensure holistic development.
  • It also aims to incorporate the teaching of vocational skills within the school curriculum to augment innovation, adaptability, and productivity.
  • Similar internship opportunities to learn vocational subjects will also be available to students throughout grades 6 to 12
  • Students from grades 6 to 8 will also be encouraged to participate in a '10-day bagless period', wherein they may intern with local vocational experts.
